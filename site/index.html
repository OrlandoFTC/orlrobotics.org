Orlando Robotics Foundation, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization focused on middle- and high-school STEM education using youth robotics programs.

We know that the best way to learn is by doing, with hands-on experiences and an atmosphere that gets students excited about learning. That's why we support educational robotics through FIRST® and their FIRST Tech Challenge program.

Our Mission:

Educating students in the areas of science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and the arts, and supporting educational robotics programs in the state of Florida.

How does it work?

We focus on amplifying the impact of existing robotics programs in Central Florida. This includes organizing events, mentoring teams, recruiting volunteers, and managing finances.

Every season provides a new challenge for the students. Every team provides a diverse set of opportunities for students to learn. Every event is a celebration of the students' hard work.

See the Challenges

  • FIRST is a worldwide organization, founded by Dean Kamen, focused on STEM education through youth robotics programs.
  • FIRST Tech Challenge is a program for middle- and high-school students with seasons running from September to April.
  • With the help of partner organizations, sponsors, and local volunteers, we facilitate FIRST Tech Challenge events in Central Florida.
